Prompt Library

Manage, organize, and deploy your AI prompts across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini with the industry's most sophisticated library system.

The Toolbar

The top toolbar is your primary control center for finding and organizing content:

SEARCH

Instant filtering across prompt titles and content bodies. RTL text is detected automatically for proper display.

SORTING

Organize by Newest First, Most Used, or Alphabetical to keep your favorites at your fingertips.

Organization & Folders

Use the Sidebar to navigate through your collections:

  • Custom Folders: Create color-coded folders for specific projects or clients.
  • Favorites: One-click "Star" to move prompts into your high-priority list.
  • Recents: Your last 10 used prompts are tracked automatically for quick reuse.
  • Recycle Bin: Deleted prompts are stored here for 7 days before permanent removal. [Pro Feature]

Bulk Actions

Select multiple prompts (Shift + Click) to trigger the Contextual Action Bar. From here, you can:

Move To

Relocate multiple prompts to a new folder simultaneously.

Delete

Remove selected prompts with a single confirmation.

Cloud Sync: Your library is currently Synced. Changes made here will be available on all your devices.
